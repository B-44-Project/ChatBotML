FAQ
  • How to register?

    To register, please follow these simple steps:

    1. Navigate to our website's homepage.
    2. Look for the "Register" or "Get-Started" button. It's usually located in the top-right corner of the page.
    3. Click on the "Register" or "Sign Up" button.
    4. Fill out the required information in the registration form. This typically includes your name, email address, phone number and a secure password.
    5. Once you've filled out the form, click the "Submit" or "Register" button.
    6. Congratulations! You're now registered and can log in using your credentials.

  • How to change my password

    To reset your password, follow these steps:

    1. Visit our website and click on the "Sign In" or "Account" link.
    2. On the login page, find and click on the "Forgot Password" or "Reset Password" link.
    3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
    4. You'll receive an email with OTP. Enter and follow next steps.
    5. Follow the prompts to create a new password for your account.
    6. Once you've successfully reset your password, use the new password to log in.

    If you encounter any issues during this process, please contact our customer support team at customersupport@gmail.com for further assistance.

  • I havent recieved OTP for change of password

    If you haven't received the OTP for password reset, please follow these steps:

    1. Check your Spam or Junk folder: Sometimes, emails containing OTPs may be filtered as spam. Please check your Spam or Junk folder in your email account.
    2. Wait for a few minutes: OTPs usually arrive promptly, but there might be a slight delay. Wait for a few minutes and check your email again.
    3. Resend the OTP: On the password reset page, there is often an option to resend the OTP. Click on the "Resend OTP" or a similar option.
    4. Verify your email address: Ensure that the email address you entered for password reset is correct. If not, go back and double-check the email address you provided.
    5. heck your internet connection: Ensure that your device is connected to the internet properly. A stable internet connection is essential for receiving emails.
    6. Contact customer support: If you've tried the above steps and still haven't received the OTP, please contact our customer support team at customersupport@gmail.com. They will assist you in resolving the issue and help you regain access to your account.

    Remember, security is our top priority, and we appreciate your patience as we work to ensure the safety of your account.
