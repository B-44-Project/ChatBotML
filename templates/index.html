FAQs

What is ChatBoT ?

A chatbot is a computer program using artificial intelligence to simulate conversation. Deployed in messaging apps and websites, it assists users by answering queries, providing information, and automating tasks. Chatbots enhance customer service, streamline interactions, and find application in various industries for improved efficiency and accessibility.

Why should we promote

Promoting chatbots for FAQs streamlines customer support, offering instant responses and freeing up human resources. They enhance user experience, providing efficient and accessible information. Chatbots optimize operations, reduce response times, and contribute to overall customer satisfaction, making them a valuable tool for effective and automated FAQ interactions.

How is it different

Compared to general chatbots, FAQ-specific ones are specialized for efficiently addressing common queries. They prioritize streamlined responses, automation of repetitive tasks, and optimizing user interactions with specific information. This targeted approach enhances their effectiveness in handling frequently asked questions compared to broader-purpose chatbots.
